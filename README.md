Blood Bank App<br>
The Blood Bank App is a simple and user-friendly mobile application designed to help users easily donate, request, and check available blood stock. It allows users to find the nearest blood centers, manage their profile, and access important information quickly. The app improves communication between donors, hospitals, and blood banks, making the blood donation process faster and more organized.<br>
